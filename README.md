## 💀 The DM's Arsenal
* **Weaponized Ambience:** Don't just tell them a monster is coming. Hit the **BOSS MODE** button. The room instantly bleeds red, the particles go frantic, and the ambient tension spikes to lethal levels. Watch their initiative plummet as you take control of their pulse.
* **The Chronos Clock:** Set a countdown timer for their "ritual casting" or their "desperate search for the exit." When the timer hits 10 seconds, it pulses a blood-red warning beacon. Players *hate* the clock. It is your favorite toy.
* **Initiative Sort-O-Matic:** One click to sort the entire combat order from highest to lowest. No more "Wait, whose turn is it?" lulls. Keep the blood pumping.
* **The Codex (Lore Archive):** Your personal digital grimoire. Keep your secret NPC backstories, hidden plot hooks, and legendary loot tables indexed and ready to deploy. 
* **The Arcana Soundboard:** Trigger immediate, jarring sound effects (monsters, doors, magic impacts) with a single tap.
