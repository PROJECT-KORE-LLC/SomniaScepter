SOMNIAVIBES FOUNDER EDITION
A local-first vibe builder for weird little brains.

WHAT THIS IS
SomniaVibes lets you make a tiny room out of pictures, sounds, glow, sparkles, color, and motion.

CORE PROMISE
No account.
No feed.
No ads.
No cloud.
No subscription.
Your vibe is yours.

HOW TO USE
1. Open index.html in a browser.
2. Tap Build a Vibe.
3. Use the big buttons:
   - Picture: upload a background or choose a built-in one
   - Sound: add a custom sound or use a built-in tone
   - Sparkle: choose particles
   - Color: choose a palette
   - Motion: choose gentle movement
   - Save: save the vibe locally
   - Pack: export/import .somniavibe files
4. Tap Record to hide the UI.
5. Use your device screen recorder to capture a short loop.

PACK / UNPACK
Pack the Vibe downloads a .somniavibe file.
That file can include custom pictures and custom sounds.
Send it however you want: email, Discord, Drive, USB, itch community, etc.
The receiver opens SomniaVibes and taps Unpack a Vibe.

PRIVACY
SomniaVibes stores your current vibe and saved vibes in browser local storage.
Nothing is uploaded by this app.
If you share a .somniavibe file, you are choosing to share whatever picture/sound data is packed inside that file.

MOBILE NOTES
This first founder build is mobile-first and simple by default.
Advanced tuning is hidden behind the Simple/Advanced button.

INSTALLING AS A PWA
Upload these files to a static host, then visit the URL in your browser.
Use your browser's install/add-to-home-screen option.
